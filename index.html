About Me

My name is Ezra Carriere. I am a student in the Fanshawe College, Interactive Media Design program. Through this program, I have learned many skills in the art of web design and have become quite passionate about it. Some of my other passions include film and sports. Movies have always been something I have been very interested in, and now they have become something I use frequently for inspiration in my designs. I believe film can be the most visually intriguing form of art; this allows me to have a near endless amount of inspiration to use when thinking of design concepts. Growing up being constantly involved in sports is the thing I believe to have made me the type of person I am today when it comes to my work. Being able to effectively communicate with others and the dedication to do something well and always giving my all towards it are both things I learned from sport that are influential on every aspect of my life now.
